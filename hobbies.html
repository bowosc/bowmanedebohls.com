My Hobbies

Magic: The Gathering
I play the popular card game Magic: The Gathering (a little too much). I spend most of my time playing EDH, brewing Modern, losing at Oathbreaker, and drafting my Cube.

I'm a certified Level 1 Tournament Judge, meaning that I oversee events and manage the complicated rules & policy interactions that occur in tournament gameplay. I've worked at several events in the greater Seattle area, including two ComicCons and a $20K RCQ / ReCQ at MXP Tacoma. (MXP had over 400 players in each RCQ / ReCQ!)

I’ve also competed in regional and subregional tournaments, and have two top 8 (and one top 16) placements in Modern Regional Championship Qualifiers.
